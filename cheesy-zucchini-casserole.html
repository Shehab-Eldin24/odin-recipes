Cheesy Zucchini Casserole

Description:

Layers of thinly sliced zucchini are baked in a creamy, cheesy blend of sharp cheddar and gooey mozzarella, with hints of garlic and herbs throughout. This comforting casserole is golden and bubbly on top, with a soft, savory center that melts in your mouth. It's the perfect balance of light garden-fresh zucchini and rich, cheesy indulgence

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bread, cubed
  • Â¼ cup melted butter
  • 2 cups cubed zucchini
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt or to taste
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place bread cubes in a medium bowl and pour melted butter over top. Add zucchini, onion, egg, garlic.
  3. Transfer the mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish and top with cheese.
  4. Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Uncover the dish and bake for another 30 minutes.
